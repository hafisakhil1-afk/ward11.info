About

A voice for Kannothpalli in the Municipal Council.

Shamseena MK is the elected councillor representing Ward 11 (Kannothpalli) in the Thalassery Municipality, Kannur District, Kerala. As a proud member of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML), she was chosen by the residents of Kannothpalli to be their voice in local governance.


A ward councillor's role touches the everyday lives of residents — from ensuring roads are repaired and drains are clean, to making sure street lights work and water supply is uninterrupted. Shamseena MK is committed to being accessible, responsive, and dedicated to the welfare of every household in Ward 11.

"Development is not just about infrastructure — it is about dignity. Every resident of Kannothpalli deserves clean streets, clean water, and a councillor who listens."

— Shamseena MK

Responsibilities

What your councillor works on

As your elected representative, Shamseena MK works across all aspects of local civic life in Ward 11.

🛣️
Roads & Footpaths

Repair and maintenance of local roads, footpaths, and street surfaces within Ward 11.

🚰
Water Supply

Ensuring reliable drinking water supply and resolving pipeline issues for all households.

💡
Street Lighting

Installation and maintenance of street lights to keep Kannothpalli safe after dark.

🌊
Drainage

Cleaning and improving drainage systems to prevent flooding and waterlogging.

🏘️
Housing & Welfare

Helping residents access government housing schemes and welfare programmes.

📋
Complaints & Grievances

Receiving and resolving resident complaints at the municipal level — your concerns matter.

🌳
Sanitation & Cleanliness

Waste management, public cleanliness, and maintaining a healthy environment in the ward.

🤝
Community Development

Supporting local events, cultural activities, and the social well-being of Ward 11 residents.
