About

A counsellor who truly listens.

Based in the beautiful coastal town of Thalassery, Kerala, I am a dedicated counsellor committed to walking alongside individuals on their journey toward emotional well-being, clarity, and personal growth.


With a warm, non-judgmental approach, I work with individuals, families, and young people facing life's challenges — whether it's anxiety, grief, relationship difficulties, or simply finding one's way through a difficult time.


Every session is a confidential, safe space where your story matters and your healing is the priority. I believe deeply in the strength and resilience that lives within every person I have the honour of working with.
